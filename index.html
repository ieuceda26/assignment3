Discover Your Favorite Meals

We're going to tackle some local establishments near campus and see what they have to offer. They are a great option for students looking for a quick bite or a sit-down meal. Walking distance friendly! (15-20 minutes) But let's go ahead and get into it already.

Happy Bowl

It is located at 1201 W Gate City Blvd. You can find it right in front of the kaplan center. They are an Asian fusion restaurant known for thier Japanese and Taiwanese inspired dishes. Open daily from 11am to 9:30pm on weekdays and 2pm to 9pm on weekends.

Hibachi Steak and Shrimp

Steak and shrimp cooked to your liking served with choice of white or fried rice. Offered in small or large.

Salmon Poke Bowl

Fresh salmon served over white rice with your choice of toppings and sauces.

Kimchi Noodle Bowl

Spicy kimchi served over noodles with your choice of protein and toppings.

Don Gala Pizzeria

Located at 1101 W Gate City Blvd, Don Gala is a local pizza joint known for their delicious and affordable pizzas. They offer a variety of toppings and crust options, making it a great choice for pizza lovers. Open daily from 1pm to 10pm, closed on Sundays.

Cheese Pizza

A classic cheese pizza with a crispy crust and gooey cheese.

Cheesesteak

A delicious cheesesteak with melted cheese and choice of side.

BBQ Chicken Pizza

A BBQ chicken pizza topped with barbeque sauce and melted cheese.

Bagner and Alexa's Breakfast and Lunch

\

Bagner and Alexa's is a mexican inspired restaurant that serve a variety of breakfast dishes and lunch options. They are located at 1618 Spring Garden St. A very popular spot for students and Greensboro residents alike. They are open daily from 7am-3pm. They closer at 8pm on Thursdays and Fridays.

El Rey Omelette

Comes with green peppers, onions, tomatoes, mushrooms, mozzarella cheese, shredded chicken with green salsa. Topped with avocado and crema.

French Toast

Dusted with powdered sugar and choice of fruit.

Torta Cubana

A grilled torta filled with carne asada, salchicha, chorizo, mozzarella cheese, ham, 1 egg, jalepenos, crema, lettuce, tomato, and avocado.

Extra Information

Don Gala and Happy bowl do accept flex for students. They also have daily specials that you can take advantage of. As for Bagner's they do have certain meals that are catered towards students that are discounted. They also have daily specials that you can check out. I've tried the majority of these dishes and I recommend all three establishments. Bagner's is definetely my favorite tho! Please go check them out.
